About Me

I’m a product designer and front-end developer whose goal is to help people redefine the limits of who they are and what they can do.

My greatest strength is rapidly learning new technology to tackle any business or personal challenge. Equal parts logic and creativity, I’m just as comfortable building web pages as leading Excel workshops.

I started out in edtech working in human resources for 2U. While researching HR and project management applications, I realized I had strong ideas about the user experience I wanted for my team.

A designer by nature but not by training, I turned to the Product and Design track at Startup Institute. I finished the program in December 2013.
