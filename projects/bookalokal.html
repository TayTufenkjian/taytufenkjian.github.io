Bookalokal Photo Sharing

At Startup Institute, I joined a cross-functional team to design additional features for the food experience platform of a partner company called Bookalokal.

I owned the photo sharing designs. The task: how could guests and hosts use the Bookalokal platform to share photos from their events?

We started with some basic user flow.

Next up, wireframes. No better way to learn Balsamiq.

Then we learned about InVision for prototyping and had too much fun adding hotspots.

The final product was a clean set of final mockups. I had never used Photoshop before, but it was time to learn quickly.
